About US

  • St. Peter Claver English Medium Pre & Primary School is one of the best Schools in Tanzania. Its premises are located at Bugene near Nyakasana Parish, 2Km from Omurushaka small town.

  • St. Peter Claver English Medium Pre & Primary School is owned by the Kayanga Catholic Diocese. The school follows Tanzanian Educational Curriculum under The Ministry of Education, Science Technology and Vocation Training. The school is found in Bugene Ward, Karagwe District Kagera Region about 2 km from Omurushaka small town.

  • Kayanga Catholic Diocese has invested enormous resources in St. Peter Claver EMP/S in order to train the young generationto become God fearing and Patriotic builders of the Nation. The School renders quality Education at an affordable cost in a tranquil environment with courses in Pre-Primary (Nursery) and Primary which is from standard 1 – 7.

  • St. Peter Claver EMP/S offers Pre-Primary one and Pre-Primary two (PP1&PP2). The Pre-Primary Scholar shave both boarding and day provisions whereas pupils from standard 1 – 7 are compulsorily boarding scholars.

  • St. Peter Claver English Medium Pre & Primary School registers pupils from all Regions in Tanzania, East Africa and other parts of the World. St. Peter Claver EMP/S being the Catholic School admits pupils of other Religious Ideologies both Boys and Girls.

  • The School provides the resources for pupils with diverse interests and abilities as much as needed. We have exceptional academic records and are renowned for the excellent performances of our pupils. We have experienced, qualified and committed staff members who educate and inspire. You cannot regret to join us.
